About Luke

The Agent at the Center of It All

Luke Richardson lives at Snoqualmie Pass — the geographic heart of the I-90 corridor — which means he doesn't just serve these markets, he knows them from the inside out. From Seattle's neighborhoods to the high desert of Ellensburg, Luke is uniquely positioned to guide buyers and sellers across the full spectrum of Washington State real estate.

A Windermere agent with deep roots in the region, Luke brings market expertise, sharp negotiation skills, and genuine local knowledge to every transaction — no matter which side of the mountains you're on.
